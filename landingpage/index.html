50 years of serving communities

Our journey began in 1971 amidst the vibrant streets of Mumbai’s bustling Colaba neighborhood. It was here that Café N More first opened its doors, offering an array of freshly roasted coffee, teas, and spices from around the globe for our customers to enjoy at home. Our name pays homage to classic literature, reflecting the rich heritage of the early coffee traders. A decade later, a young Indian named Rutvik Raut walked through our doors, enchanted by the exceptional quality of our coffee. After joining our team in 1982, Rajesh's travels led him to the vibrant coffeehouses of Milan in 1983. Inspired by Italy’s warm and artistic coffee culture, he returned to Mumbai, and by 1987, Café N More evolved into a beloved coffeehouse, marked by our signature green aprons and a renewed commitment to creating a welcoming coffee experience for all.
